You may use the name **AgentSmith** without explicit written permission under the following conditions:

You **cannot** use the name **AgentSmith**, any variation of it, or the project logo for the following activities without explicit, prior written authorization from The Owner:

* **Commercial Products or Services:** You may not use the name to market, brand, or sell a commercial product, SaaS platform, cloud hosting service, or support service (e.g., selling a service called *"Managed AgentSmith"* or *"AgentSmith Cloud"*).
